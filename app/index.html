flybox

'flybox.io' Email and Communication Service

- gGitHub Organization flybox.io

Flybox is a communication service experiment that replaces the inconvenience and outdated technology of email with a thread that can utilize the potential of modern browsers. The service can be used with any existing email client and does not require recipients to have a flybox account.

The service uses Node.js, AWS-sdk, Nodemailer, JWT, MongoDB, Grunt, and Mocha on the backend with AngularJS and SASS on the frontend. The frontend and backend communicate with Express and Socket.IO. The entire UI is custom made, with zero framework dependencies. My team and I built a working demo—including launching a live site with a complimentary Chrome Extension—in less than a week.
